Overall Performance
Q1 of 14 KPI · Overall Satisfaction Score
Overall, how satisfied are you with The Lanes Group as a staffing supplier?
1 = Very dissatisfied  ·  10 = Exceptional — couldn't ask for more
Q2 of 14 KPI · Candidate Quality Index
How would you rate the quality and work-readiness of the candidates we supply?
Consider attitude, skill match, ability to follow instructions, and overall suitability to the role
Fill Rate & Speed to Fill
Q3 of 14 KPI · Fill Rate %
What percentage of your shift or vacancy requests do we successfully fill?
Fill rate is a primary DHL PSL metric — the % of orders fulfilled vs total orders placed
Q4 of 14 KPI · Time to Fill
How quickly do we typically fulfil urgent or same-day cover requests?
Think about emergency cover — a no-show at 5am, sudden volume spike, or bank holiday demand
Compliance, H&S & Onboarding
Q5 of 14 KPI · Compliance & Audit Score
How would you rate our workers' compliance documentation accuracy?
Right to work, ID verification, PPE, induction sign-off, training certs, DBS where required
Q6 of 14 KPI · H&S Induction Readiness
Do our workers arrive correctly inducted, H&S briefed, and operationally ready?
Are site rules, manual handling, PPE, and emergency procedures communicated before day one?
Retention & Attendance
Q7 of 14 KPI · Worker Retention Rate
What is your experience of worker retention from our placements?
DHL benchmarks 4-week and 12-week retention as a core PSL performance measure
Q8 of 14 KPI · Absence / No-Show Rate
How would you rate our workers' punctuality and attendance reliability?
No-shows and late arrivals directly impact throughput, SLA performance, and your team's planning
Account Management & Service
Q9 of 14 KPI · Account Management Responsiveness
How responsive and proactive is The Lanes Group account management team?
Speed of response, escalation handling, site visit frequency, proactive communication
Q10 of 14 KPI · Peak Period Fill Performance
Have we delivered during your depot's peak demand periods?
e.g. Christmas, Black Friday, Q4 surge, bank holiday cover, last-minute volume spikes
PSL Benchmarking & Value
Q11 of 14 KPI · PSL Supplier Ranking
Compared to other staffing suppliers on your PSL, where do we rank?
This is one of the key data points DHL use to review agency performance across their depot network
Q12 of 14 KPI · Business Impact Score
If The Lanes Group were removed from your PSL, how would it impact your depot?
The single most important metric DHL use when deciding whether to retain a supplier on the PSL
Q13 of 14 KPI · Value for Money / Margin Competitiveness
How would you rate The Lanes Group on value for money and commercial competitiveness?
Consider charge rate transparency, margin fairness, and value relative to quality of service delivered
Q14 of 14 Open Feedback
Share a specific example of when we went above and beyond — or an area you'd like us to improve.
Real examples carry huge weight. The more specific the better — this goes directly to our senior leadership team and forms part of the DHL case study.
